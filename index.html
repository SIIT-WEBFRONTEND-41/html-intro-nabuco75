VENETO

Veneto is the best part of Italy. If you like ancient history you can visit the coloseum from Verona. Do you have passion for middle Age history, then you have plenty of choices. Feeling out of inspiration, need a muse? Go to Arqa Petrarca and meet the muse Laura! There you will visit the place where Petrarca spent his last years. Not a fun of poetry? Try to understand the inovation that Giotto brought to religious painting. Motion and emotions take place of byzantine technique. Visit Scrovegni Chappel! Maybe you like architecture? Then go and visit Saint Antonio'Cathedral from Padua. Maybe you want to see the perfect roman empire legacy in building a piazza? Well, go and visit Montagnana, one of the most beautiful walled cities in Italy. Piazza Majore is the perfect example of roman urbanism. Large space with acces to fountain, to church and a focal point. Fun of nature? How about a visit to Lake Garda? Need enterteinment? Well, why don't you spend a day in Gardaland? Are you a romantic type? Venice is calling to you! Why wait? Come to the best part of Italy!

Places to visit:

  • Venice
  • Padua
  • Arqua Petrarca
  • Montagnana
  • Verona
  • Gardaland
  • Villa Pisani
  • Villa Barbarigo
